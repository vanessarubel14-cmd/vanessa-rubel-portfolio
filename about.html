About

Creative strategy, from every angle

I’m Vanessa, and I’m focused on brand and creative strategy for fashion and lifestyle. I’m interested in how brands can build deeper connections with their core audience while continuing to grow. I look at what people’s taste and behavior actually signal, then turn those insights into stronger decisions across product, content, collaborations, and experiences.

I bring a creative eye, business training, and a high-output work style, so I can move quickly from the central idea to the finished work.

What's next

I’m building toward brand and creative roles where I can help fashion and lifestyle brands make culturally informed decisions, create stronger work, and build deeper relationships with their audiences.
